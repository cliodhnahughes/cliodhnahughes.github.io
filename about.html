About

I am a first year PhD student in the UKRI Centre for Doctoral Training (CDT) in Speech and Language Technologies at the University of Sheffield. My PhD research is on singing voice banking and conversion, and is supervised by Professor Guy Brown, Dr Ning Ma and Professor Nikki Dibben. Alongside speech technology, I am also interested in language evolution.
